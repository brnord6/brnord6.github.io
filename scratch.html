Why Soccer?

Soccer is more than a sport — it's a universal language spoken in every country on earth. The pace, the skill, the drama of a last-minute goal... nothing compares. My favorite club is Manchester City, and my favorite player is Erling Haaland — arguably the most clinical striker of his generation.

Manchester City

Founded in 1880, Manchester City FC has become one of the most dominant clubs in Premier League history, winning multiple titles under Pep Guardiola's management.
